## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

These templates follow a minimalist design philosophy:

- **Clean & Scannable** - Easy for recruiters to quickly understand your skills
- **Lots of Whitespace** - Content breathes and doesn't feel cramped
- **Max Content Width** - ~900-1000px centered for optimal readability
- **System Fonts** - Fast loading, no external font dependencies
- **Subtle Animations** - Smooth transitions without being distracting
- **Professional Layout** - One-page design with clear sections
